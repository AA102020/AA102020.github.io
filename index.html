Biography

Abdul Ahmed is a researcher and strategist working across responsible AI, institutional risk, computational science, banking, defense technology, technology policy, and the digital economy. His work focuses on making AI-enabled and data-driven systems reliable, explainable, auditable, and governable in institutional settings.

His current research centers on three connected problems: reliability evaluation for AI-generated analytic deliverables, inspection regimes for the institutional standing of AI-generated work products, and policy dilemmas in algorithmic command and defense AI governance. He is affiliated with Informatics AI in Washington, DC, and maintains scholarly profiles through ORCID and Google Scholar.

Selected Works

Reliability of AI-Generated Analytic Deliverables

Reliability of AI-Generated Analytic Deliverables: A Stochastic Workflow Evaluation Procedure. A. Ahmed. Working manuscript, May 2026.

SSRN: https://ssrn.com/abstract=6789343

Develops a stochastic workflow evaluation procedure for AI-generated analytic deliverables, centered on repeated-run diagnostics, source-role traceability, workflow-choice discipline, task-conditioned comparison, reproducibility, reviewability, and use-specific decision thresholds.

Inspection Regimes and Institutional Standing

Decipimur Specie Recti - Inspection Regimes: How Generative AI Deliverables Become Valid and Admissible Work Products. A. Ahmed. Working paper, May 2026. DOI: 10.5281/zenodo.20268016.

SSRN: https://ssrn.com/abstract=6776300

Develops an STS account of how AI-generated deliverables acquire institutional standing through registration, containment, inspection, classification, authorization, controlled circulation, maintenance, and reinspection.

Algorithmic Command and Defense AI Governance

Algorithmic Command: Policy Dilemmas for the Age of AI Warfare. A. Ahmed.

Examines AI-enabled military decision systems, accountability, opacity, meaningful human control, autonomous decision-making, policy design, and governance at machine speed.
